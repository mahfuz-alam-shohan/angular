History

Year 1954. In Greater Chittagong, there was a silent procession to stymie the Prophet's lovers. The inactivity of saints haters. In such a transitional period, due to the need of time, Qutbul Auliya Hazratul Allama Alhaj Hafeez Qari Syed Ahmad Shah Sirikoti (R.H.) established Jamea Ahmadia Sunnia Alia in the Sholashahar of Chittagong based on Maslake Ala Hazrat. Since its inception, for more than half a century, this religious educational institution has been making a great contribution to the service of religion, nation and madhhab in the subcontinent, crossing the borders of the country. Under the patronage of the worthy successor of the founder, Allama Syed Muhammad Tayyab Shah (R.A.) and the present descendants Huzur Qiblah Maddazilu-hul Ali, the Jamea's fame is world-wide today. This is as bright as the sun.
