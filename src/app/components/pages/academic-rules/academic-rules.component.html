Academic Rules

Discipline, Manners, Politeness
The students are required to abide by the rules and regulations of the institution and behave according to the norms and manners of our culture and society. If any student fails to adhere to these and defy the rules of discipline, he will be dealt with proper punishment and this may lead to expulsion from the institution.
Leave of absence:
Students are expected to be punctual in attending their classes, which is the basic source of their learning. In case of sickness or any other valid reasons the students should submit written application to the principal with comments from his parent/legal guardian and leave will be granted. In case of absence of the child, the Principal should be informed with a letter from the parents. The doctors must certify a long absence for sickness. No leave will be granted without the sanction of the principal. In case of urgent requirement, leave will be granted on submission of excuse letter from parents or guardians.
Meeting and co-ordination-guardian, teachers & authority of this institution
The parents send their children for learning and preparation of lessons in the institution. The institutions expects that the parents will see their wards prepare their lessons at home on their own as per direction and guidance given by the Madrasha teachers. Parents/Guardians would be kept updated with pertaining information regarding the performance of the students. Suggestions on problems and issues discussed in the meeting as offered by the parents will always be welcomed for improvement of the institution. We expect the parent/guardian to see us as and when he seems so. We expect whole-hearted co-operation from the parents towards achieving the aims and objectives of the institution.
