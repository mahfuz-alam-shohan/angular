At a Glance

Based on the ideology of Iman-e-Ahle Sunnat Ala Hajrat. Sayedul Aulia Sayed Ahmad Shah Sirikoti (R) uttered a most valuable verdict at the time of the foundation laying ceremony of Jamea-“Yea Madrasah Ka Buniad Masalke Ala-Hajrat Per Dhala Geya”. So, Jamea started its onward journey with Hajrat Allamah Wakar Uddin Brelavi, a renowned Alem-e-deen of this sub continent and one of the pillars of Maslke Ala Hajrat in 1956. Then Shahebjada Gause Zaman Sayed Muhammad Tyeb Shah (R.) selected Allamah Nasarullah Khan, a note-worthy personality among the followers of the ideology of Hajrat. Therefore all the academic and administrative activities of Jamea had been being performed according to the maslake Ala Hajrat in the practical sense. This Jamea is the first institution that attempted to establish the doctrine of Ala Hajrat in Bangladesh and now undoubtedly plays the principal role in leading Maslak-e-Ala Hajrat around Bangladesh under the Principal.
