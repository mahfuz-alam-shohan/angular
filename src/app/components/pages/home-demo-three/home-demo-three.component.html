Welcome Message

Assalam Alaikum Wa Rahmatullahi Wa Barakatuhu.

Welcome to Jamea Ahmadia Sunnia Kamil Madrasah. May Allah bestow upon you Jaza E Khair for choosing to provide your child an education that will give a sound foundation for the betterment of his Duniya and Akhira.

Children are truly a blessing and ammanh from Allah Az Wa Jal and providing them with the right upbringing and education is parent's foremost duty.

The Holy Prophet Mohammad (Sallallahu Alaihe Wa Sallam) said - “To teach one's child manners is better than giving alms in charity in the way of Allah.” (Tirmidhi Sharif)

Hazrat Allama Syed Tayyab Shah (RAH) Said - “Work, save Islam, save Religion, create true Islamic Scholars.”

Jamea Ahmadia Sunnia Kamil Madrasah imparts education in an Islamic environment. Islamic principles and teachings are integral parts of school curriculum. As students of Jamea Madrasah, student first learn to love the Creator Allah Subhan Wa Ta'ala, Rasulullah Sallallahu Alaihe Wa Sallam And Wali-Allah before learning Mathematics, Science or Social Studies etc.. Reading of Holy Quran, Hadith, Fiqh, Tafsir Duas are lessons that are conducted on a daily basis. Emphasis on co-curricular activities such as P.T And Educational Excursions, ensures all round development of the student.

Well qualified teachers and staff at Jamea Ahmadia Sunnia Kamil Madrasah maintain a family like warm and embracing atmosphere and strives to acknowledge each student's physical, mental and emotional potential without any discrimination or bias. Facilitating such a loving and encouraging atmosphere propels the student with leaps and bounds on his academic journey.

Jamea Ahmadia Sunnia Kamil Madrasah understands the struggle that parents go through to provide an Islamic upbringing to their children in this turbulent times, hence Bi Iznillah it arranges workshops sessions for parents on a regular basis.

Ever growing and evolving Jamea Ahmadia Sunnia Kamil Madrasah strives to provide education to student that will Inshallah Help Them To Attain The Best In This World And The Hereafter Too.

May Allah Accept All Our Deeds And Keep Us Rooted On Sirat Al Mustaqeem.

Aameen.
