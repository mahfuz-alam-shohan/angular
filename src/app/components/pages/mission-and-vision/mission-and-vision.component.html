Mission and Vision

Jamea Ahmadia Sunnia Kamil Madrasah to create Haqqani Rabbani scholars who believe in the creed of Ahle Sunnat wal Jama’at the main outline of Islam, trusting in Maslake Ala Hazrat, enlivened by the love of the Prophet (SM). Through them, to create fear of Allah, love of the Prophet (SM) and devotion to the saints and perform the service of religion. In our activities to fulfill this holy purpose and goal, we seek the immense grace of Allah and His beloved Habib (SM), the eternal care of Auliyae Keram and the overall cooperation and sympathy of the Prophet-loving Muslims.
